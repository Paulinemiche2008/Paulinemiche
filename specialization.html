Music Specialization Journey

Throughout the years, we have learned different forms of music. In choir and rondalla, one of the popular songs that we always sing is **Kruhay**. We also perform a folk song medley with it.

In our **Rondalla**, we learned many musical pieces, including **Mauli Gid Ako sa Antique, Probinsyana Gwapa, Mamang Sorbetero,** and many more.

For us music majors, music is not just about singing and playing instruments. **Music brings people together, unites us, and offers endless learning opportunities.** I am proud and happy to be part of this program.
