Elementary - Where Fun and Memorable Memories Begin

Transitioning from Preschool to Elementary School, learning became a bit more structured and defined. We were introduced to various subjects like Math, Science, Mother Tongue, Arts, and many more. Each subject was taught by amazing, enthusiastic teachers who enlightened me and fueled my curiosity. I found lifelong friends, created strong bonds, and made unforgettable moments. The silliness and joy of elementary school remain in my heart and mind.
